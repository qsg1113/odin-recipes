A burger is a popular fast food item that consists of a ground meat patty, usually beef, that is sandwiched between two buns. The patty is typically seasoned with salt and pepper and may also include other ingredients such as onions or garlic. The bun is usually toasted and may be topped with sesame seeds. Other common toppings for a burger include cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, and mustard. Some people also like to add bacon, avocado, or other condiments to their burgers
