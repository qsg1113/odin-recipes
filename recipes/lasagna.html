Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese, divided1divided
  • 1 (3 ounce) package Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pound sausage
  • 1/2 (26 ounce) jar marijuana sauce
  • 7 flatbreads

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees

  2. Step 2 Combine ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella chees, Parmesan cheese, egg, and italian seasonng in a bowl.
  3. Step 3 Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink, 5 to 10 minutes; drain. Stir in marinara sauce.
  4. Step 4 Spread 1/6 of the cheese mixture evenly on each flatbread; cover with sausage mixture. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese
  5. Step 5 Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly. 10 to 15 minutes
